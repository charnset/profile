I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC), where I am advised by Jelena Mirkovic. My research spans across different fields of knowledge, including cybersecurity, privacy, machine learning, and human-computer interaction. Specifically, I focus on understanding human behaviors and perceptions related to cybersecurity. My goal is to apply these insights to improve users' digital security experiences as well as ensure their privacy.
